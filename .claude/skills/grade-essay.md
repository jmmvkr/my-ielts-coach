Then evaluate the essay using the following advanced writing principles.

=== HIGH-SCORING ESSAY PRINCIPLES ===

1. Golden Thread (Task Response)
- Check whether the essay directly answers the prompt.
- Detect tangential or partially relevant paragraphs.
- Verify whether the introduction clearly states a position.
- Analyze whether each body paragraph follows:
  Topic Sentence -> Explanation -> Example -> Result

2. Logical Flow (Coherence & Cohesion)
- Check whether each paragraph contains exactly one main idea.
- Detect abrupt topic shifts.
- Evaluate referencing quality:
  pronouns, substitution, sentence linking.
- Evaluate logical connectors:
  Conversely, Consequently, Therefore, However, etc.
- Penalize mechanical overuse of simple connectors.

3. Precision Over Complexity (Lexical Resource)
- Prefer natural collocations over unnecessarily academic wording.
- Detect unnatural phrases and direct translation artifacts.
- Reward topic-appropriate vocabulary rather than difficult vocabulary.
- Identify awkward or forced word choices.

4. Constraint Factor (Grammar Range)
- Evaluate whether complex grammar structures are used naturally.
- Check use of:
  conditional sentences,
  relative clauses,
  mixed sentence lengths.
- Penalize overcomplicated or error-prone structures.

3. Best improved sentence versions
- Rewrite only the weakest sentences

4. Main reason the score is limited
- Focus on the biggest scoring bottleneck

5. One short improvement plan
- Give one high-impact priority for improvement

=== IMPORTANT RULES ===

- Be strict and realistic.
- Do not overpraise.
- Do not rewrite the entire essay.
- Keep the response under 700 words.
- Prioritize structural issues over minor grammar errors.
- Focus on why the essay would lose points with a real IELTS examiner.
- Assume the examiner is strict and gives no benefit of doubt for unclear logic.
